The Dynamic Map is a way to detect the presence of the enemy team (humans or zombies). Tap the alert button at the bottom of the screen when you see an enemy. A marker will be placed on the map at your current location, letting your allies know that an enemy was seen in this area. The marker will change color according to its age:

  • Black: < 5 minutes old.
  • Red: < 10 minutes old.
  • Orange: < 15 minutes old.
  • Yellow: < 30 minutes old.
  • Green: < 45 minutes old.
  • Blue: < 60 minutes old.
Markers older than 60 minutes don't display on the map.
